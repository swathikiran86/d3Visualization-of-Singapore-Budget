Visualization of Singapore Budget Expenditure

As observed over the past two decades




Graph One:

Sector-wise Budget Expenditure for the years from 1999 to 2018.
Each line graph below represents the investment/expenditure done by Singapore Govt in a particular sector. Each dot over the line highlights the amount - in Million Singapore Dollars (SGD).

Graph Two:

Ministry wise expenditure under each sector for a span of 5 years is discussed below. The time scale can be selected from the below dropdown menu (Default span is "2018-2014" when selected from the graph one above). Clicking on the Sector legend of multiple line graph (graph one) we arrive at this graph. Here, can compare the amount spent by the Government on each ministry of a sector for a period of 5 year.



Graph Three:

Expenditure/Investments in each sector are made under various ministries. And each investment is further segregated into two types (Opearting and Deveopment). The two different fill colors indicate the two types of investment. Clicking on a data point on the multiple line graph (graph one) we can investigate the amount spent on each of those ministries that fall under that sector for the year clicked as shown below.
